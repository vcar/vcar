Driving Analytics Solution

vCar Driving Analytics Solution processes the data coming from the connected vehicle and generates advanced driving behavior and driving pattern reports. The data is collected and analysed with the ultimate goal to gain a full understanding of the drivers capability to adjust his driving based on driving conditions.

Car data storages

Storing collected In Vehicle Data Recorders, using a document store engine , capable of scaling petabytes of data.

Data visualization

Visualize connected car data (IVDRs) in many forms, including Charts, Maps and Graphs, using direct and abstructed forms

Driving Behavior

Automated Driver Behavior Analysis, and profiling driving styles detection, using both collected and external data (roads, weather...)

Predictive System
